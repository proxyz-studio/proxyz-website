02
The Thesis

What PROXYZ Studio actually is.

PROXYZ Studio is an operator studio. We don't sell software seats. We install a proprietary operating system called the Portal (codename: Studio OS, live at portal.proxyz.studio) into the companies we acquire, partner with, take equity in, or build for cash.

The Portal is both our internal infrastructure and the deliverable. Once installed, it becomes the company's spine: how decisions get made, how work gets routed, how memory persists across people and time, how agents do the operational work humans used to do twice. It is the difference between "we use AI" and "AI runs the company."

Core stack is operational transformation, AI and automation, EOS / Traction methodology layered with our own PROXYZ modules, and deal architecture inside the Thai network.
